Privacy Policy for Neet Study Hub

About Neet Study Hub

Neet Study Hub is an online platform dedicated to providing free study resources, notes, and guidance for students preparing for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). Our goal is to make learning accessible to everyone by offering high-quality content tailored to student needs.

We do not require users to create accounts, sign up, or sign in to access our resources. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle data collected when you use our website.

Information We Collect

When you visit Neet Study Hub, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Non-Personal Information: This includes details such as your browser type, device information, IP address, and pages you visit. This information helps us understand how users interact with our website and improve its functionality.
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: We use cookies and similar technologies to track your browsing behavior and personalize your experience on our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve our website.
  • To analyze traffic and user behavior.
  • To deliver personalized advertisements through Google AdSense.

Google AdSense

Neet Study Hub uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your interests and browsing history. These cookies allow Google and its partners to provide you with relevant ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information on how Google uses data, please refer to the Google Advertising Policies.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow us to enhance your browsing experience. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your preferences.
  • To analyze website performance and user interactions.
  • To deliver relevant ads and content.

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect some features of our website.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information collected from unauthorized access or misuse. However, please note that no online platform can guarantee absolute security. Your use of our website is at your own risk.

Third-Party Links

Neet Study Hub may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies when you visit them.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
